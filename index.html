About The Work Archive

Hey there! 👋 We're Neelam and Shruti — two passionate college students on a mission to help startups and small businesses establish their digital presence.

As students ourselves, we understand the challenges of building something from scratch. That's why we founded The Work Archive — to bridge the gap between ambitious startups and professional digital solutions.

🚀 What we do:
We help early-stage startups grow by providing:

• 💻 Full-stack web development — from MVPs to complete platforms
• 🎨 Creative brand identity — logos, posters, and thumbnails that stand out
• 📱 Social media content — engaging reels and video edits
• 🔧 Technical consulting — guiding founders through their digital journey

We believe that every great startup deserves a great digital presence. Whether you're launching your first product or looking to scale, we're here to help you make an impact.
